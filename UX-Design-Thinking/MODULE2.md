# MODULE1: Ideas generation
### Double diamond
Ideation steps based on design thinking to enable idea generation, decide what is relevant and refine the ideas and design a solution. It's like a structured brainstorming.

### How Might We... (HWM)
A simple technique to establish a common language when defining doubts about a product. Used to increase the range of solutions about your product.

### Crazy 8's
Avoid obvious ideas and exit the comfort zone, by designing 8 different ways to do the same thing, in 5 minutes.

### User flow
Graphic representation of user interacting with your product.

### Taxonomy
An exploration of various ways of categorizing content and information. Content organization helps the user to use the system more easily.

### Sitemaps
A way to represent the taxonomy of a system. A hierarchical diagram showing the structure of the site.

### Moodboard
It is a visual representation of elements and references that will help define the appearance of the product.
