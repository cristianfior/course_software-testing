# MODULE7: Success metrics
### Key Performance Indicators - KPI
Indicators that ket you to follow the operation evolution.
- Productivity indicator
- Quality indicator
- Capacity indicator
- Strategy indicator

### Net Promoter Score - NPS
A methodology created to measure the degree of user satisfaction with the company.

### Click through rate - CTR
It is the number of times a user clicked on your ad or call to action in relation to the total number of times that element was shown.

### Daily active users - DAU
Measure the user fidelization

### Churn rate
It is the number of monthly cancellation by active users

### Growth rate
It is the number of monthly new users

### Lifetime value - LTV
It's how much your customer gives you as a subscriber

### Customer acquisition cost - CAC
It is how much it costs to "buy" a customer

### Baseline
It is your actual numbers

### HEART
- Happiness
    - User satisfaction
    - NPS
- Engagement
    - User activity
    - System/APP use
- Adoption
    - New users
    - New functionalities use
    - Growth rate
- Retention
    - Percentual user actives divided per time unit
    - Churn rate
    - LTV
- Task Success
    - Used time to perform a task
    - Quantity of performed tasks
    - Erros encountered while performing a task
