# MODULE1: Products strategy
### Stakeholders
It is the strategic public. A person or grop that have insterest in your business.

### Future press release
Used when you need to showcase what the product is all about to your sponsors and stakeholders and when you want to align team work.

### Blueprint
Used when you need to improve the process of a service experience.

### Ecosystem
It is a representation of all user touchpoints with your brand or product. Seeks to identify opportunities for improvement in points that sometimes we do not care about.

### Golden path
It is the "happy way" that the user finds to carry out an activity. It's the user journey that 90% of users will encounter when using the product.

### Value proposal canvas
It is a method to define what is the product, for who it was created and how/when will be used.

### Benchmark
Identify the strengths and threats of competitors products and services. Generate consensus on a quality baseline with stakeholders. In short: a competitor analysis that clarifies features by comparing them to each other.

### First tweet
It is an exercise that simulates what a company's first tweet would look like after it became public for users, considering the 140-character limitation.

### 360 lightning talk
People chatting for a while with the aim of sharing some views.
